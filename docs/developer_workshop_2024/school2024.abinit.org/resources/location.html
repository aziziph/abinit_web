Location - Practical information

ABINIT School
Bruyères-le-Châtel, Jan. 29 – Feb. 2 2024

Bruyères-le-Châtel

The school will take place at the Très Grand Centre de Calcul (TGCC) in the TERATEC campus next to the CEA center in Bruyères-le-Châtel, France.

Please note that the access to the TGCC is submitted to conditions.

TERATEC
2 Rue de la Piquetterie
91680 Bruyères-le-Châtel
France

The site is about 40km from Paris and can be reached either by car or public transportation.

There will be a shuttle making the connection from Massy-Palaiseau station to the TGCC on Monday 29 January at 12:00. 
The specific location of the shuttle will be communicated later.
This is by far the easiest way to reach the TGCC if you are not coming by car.

From CDG airport you can take the RER B directly at the Airport, and from Orly airport you need to take the OrlyVal train first to reach the RER B in Antony. If you arrive at the Massy-Palaiseau TGV station then you can directly take the shuttle.

There will be also be a shuttle to Massy-Palaiseau on Friday leaving around 13:30 from the TGCC and reaching Massy-Palaiseau around 14:15.

Accomodation

All the participants will have the opportunity to be hosted in a single or a double room at the Belambra Dourdan (according to registration choice and availability). Breakfast and diner are included in the reservation and price.

A bus will make the link between the hotel and the training rooms every day.

Belambra Hotel (Le domaine du Normont)
Av. d'Orléans
91410 Dourdan
France
