Hackathons - Discussions

ABINIT Developer Workshop
Guidel-Plages, May 16-19, 2022
The workshop is mainly dedicated to actual implementation and decision making by the developers.
Every morning we have a session of hackathons. Divided into small groups, we concretely work on ABINIT : coding, improving the documentation, interfacing the code with other software, etc. Everyone might find satisfaction according to their level of involvement in the project.
During two afternoons, we meet to discuss and consider the future of the code: future scientific themes, dissemination and the visibility of the code, user experience improvement.
The list of hackathons and discussion topics is available for logged users (see login menu above).
