Workshop registration

ABINIT Developer Workshop
Guidel-Plages, May 16-19, 2022

Important note

The workshop is only on personal invitation.

How to register

Before you can apply for the workshop, you need to create an account, if not already done (see Login menu on the top).
If you had a login for the June 2021 workshop, you can/must reuse it. The login ID has to be the same as in june 2021 (this is not the e-mail address). It is active only if you have been contacted to participate to the workshop.

Afterwards, sign, click on the "Workshop registration" menu item again and fill in the form below.

Registration fee

The registration fee covers accomodation, full board, lectures and excursion/dinner evening in Concarneau. So, all local expenses covered. See the Practical information page for details.

The fee depends on the number of nights you spend:

  • 4 nights (from sunday may 15 to thursday may 19): 425€
  • 3 nights (from monday may 16 to thursday may 19): 375€

Invited speakers are free of charge. They have been officially invited to give a talk (see the Program page).

To pay the registration fee, please go to the Payment page.

Deadlines

  • Registration deadline: March 31st, 2022
  • Payment deadline: April 30th, 2022
