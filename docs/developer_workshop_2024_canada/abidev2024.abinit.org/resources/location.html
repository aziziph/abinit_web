The conference venue, Le Baluchon, is a vast environmentally friendly estate located in the region of Mauricie, 150 km North-East of the city of Montréal.

Accomodation:

All the participants will be hosted in a double occupancy room at Le Baluchon. 
Some single occupancy rooms are available at an extra cost.

Three meals per day are included in the reservation, from dinner on Sunday June 30th to lunch on Thursday July 4th.

Activities:

The eco-resort Le Baluchon offers many activities and equipment included with the stay:
  • forest hiking trails (35 km)
  • mountain biking
  • canoeing, kayaking ( 7 km on Rivière du Loup)
  • tennis court, volleyball
  • swimming pools (indoor and outdoor)
  • gym
  • archery, horseshoes, pétanque
  • self-guided wildlife observation walk

At an extra-cost:
  • full range of  spa treatments
  • horse back riding
  • carriage ride
  • outdoor yoga

Weather:

At the end of June, the weather is usually warm during the day, 25 Celsius, and drops to 13 Celsius at night.

Beware, the region hosts mosquitoes! They are most active from the end of the afternoon, through the night, until early morning. Be prepared in order to enjoy nature!
  • Wear clear and ample/long clothing if planning to hike in the forest.   
  • Avoid scented personal care products. 
  • Bring mosquito/insect repellent spray.
